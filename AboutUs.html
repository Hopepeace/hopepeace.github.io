Our History

The idea was generated by compus students who got really tired of spending lot of money on books and trashing it the next minit the course is over. One of those student was me. We collabourated and put all the books we have as a start. I told my friends, and friends told other friends who were able to sign up more books. Other Stuudents herd the word and started using more of the web page.

Our Goal

We are working and supporting this site in order to be able to minimize the cost books and other resources for students. Our goal is to collabourate with thousand students per year at least and easily make books, and resources exchange.

We are also working to make this happen to other colleges and universities. It is our goal to create links, share experiance and provide all the guidance needed so that other colleges and universities have this for their students.

Our Mission Statement

Books are valuable only when used. Lets share them easily and make them more valuable and accessable

Values

We value every book and resource give to us. We hope and trust that users of the book or this web site also value this and keep the books/ resource in a good condition.
